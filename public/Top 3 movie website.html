My top three movies of all time

Order does not indicate a quality ranking

1. A Clockwork Orange

A rather disturbing movie that famously caused a legal dispute with "Singing in the Rain" the musical.
A brilliant investigation into what freedom of decision truly entails, and whether or not being forced to do good is truly any good.

2. Bullet Train

A hilarious movie that encompasses a true "whodunnit" feeling to it.
Following along a hilarious cast of individuals whom all have boarded the same train for their own motives, the mystery lies in why they've all boarded the same train however.

3. The Menu

The Menu is without a doubt one of my absolute favorite Comedy Thrillers I have ever seen.
Comedy, mystery, and even a bit of spine-tingling horror all combine into a wonderful package that explores the destruction of mystery wrought onto all industries as time has gone on.
